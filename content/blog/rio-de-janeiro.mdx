Rio works when the itinerary follows the geography.

## Keep the beach as the baseline

Copacabana and Ipanema are not just beaches. They are the city’s social reset button. Use them for the first and last part of the day.

## Add one iconic view

Christ the Redeemer or Pão de Açúcar gives the trip the height it needs. One viewpoint is enough if the rest of the day is already full.

## Avoid crowding the route

Rio is at its best when you leave room for a long lunch, a sunset, or a slow transfer across the coast.

That is what makes it feel premium: not more movement, but cleaner movement.
