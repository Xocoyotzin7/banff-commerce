Chichén Itzá is best when the schedule respects the site.

## Go early

The early window is the one that gives you the most space, the best temperature, and the clearest photos. It also keeps the rest of the day open instead of overloaded.

## Build the day around one pairing

The strongest combo is the archaeological site plus one cenote stop. That pairing gives the route a clear arc: monument, water, rest.

## Keep the return elegant

After the site, avoid stacking too many stops. One lunch, one transfer, one pause back at the hotel is enough. The goal is to leave the day feeling intentional rather than exhausted.
